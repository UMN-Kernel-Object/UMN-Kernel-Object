About

University of Minnesota Kernel Object is on a mission to educate students about kernel-based software development and provide opportunities for hands-on experience with low-level computing architecture. We also foster a community for students and organizations interested in kernel development, promoting collaboration and knowledge-sharing in this exciting field.

Our documentation and all other group related resource can be found online.
